# 🕒 Comprehensive Time Module
High-precision clock parsers, delta calculation trackers, and platform delays.

### Complete Feature List:
1. `gage_time_stamp()` - Universal global UNIX timestamp.
2. `gage_time_sleep_sec(s)` - Delays execution loop by seconds.
3. `gage_time_sleep_ms(ms)` - High-speed millisecond delay engine.
4. `gage_time_current_year()` - Active platform year counter.
5. `gage_time_current_month()` - Calendar active tracking month (1-12).
6. `gage_time_current_day()` - Monthly day coordinate tracking limit.
7. `gage_time_current_hour()` - Military timeline hour parser (0-23).
8. `gage_time_current_min()` - Clock minute matrix tracker bounds.
9. `gage_time_current_sec()` - Execution tracking seconds ticker element.
10. `gage_time_is_leap_year(y)` - Modular algorithm for leap years.
11. `gage_time_days_in_month(m, y)` - Capacity calculator for month sizes.
12. `gage_time_day_of_year()` - Counts elapsed days in current loop.
13. `gage_time_day_of_week()` - Weekly vector tracking integer (0=Sun).
14. `gage_time_is_dst()` - Verification flag for regional time tracking shifts.
15. `gage_time_cpu_ticks()` - Raw performance metric ticks counter.
16. `gage_time_add_hours(t, h)` - Offsets timeline forward by hours math fields.
17. `gage_time_add_days(t, d)` - Offsets timeline forward by calendar days.
18. `gage_time_diff_seconds(t1, t2)` - Direct seconds delta equation mapping.
19. `gage_time_diff_minutes(t1, t2)` - Compares timelines down into minutes fields.
20. `gage_time_diff_hours(t1, t2)` - Compares timelines down into hours layers.
21. `gage_time_diff_days(t1, t2)` - Full calendar day separation tracking meter.
22. `gage_time_to_minutes(s)` - Math scaler translating seconds to minutes.
23. `gage_time_to_hours(s)` - Math scaler translating seconds to hours.
24. `gage_time_to_days(s)` - Math scaler translating seconds to calendar days.
25. `gage_time_clock_runtime()` - App loop longevity tracker from initialization.
26. `gage_time_make_timestamp(h, m, s)` - Bundles distinct integers inside a frame.
27. `gage_time_is_morning()` - Validates active morning timeline status.
28. `gage_time_is_afternoon()` - Validates active afternoon timeline status.
29. `gage_time_is_evening()` - Validates active evening timeline status.
30. `gage_time_is_night()` - Validates active night operational constraints status.
